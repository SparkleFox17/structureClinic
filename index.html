About

Wrinkle Reduction

Wrinkle Reduction (Botox® & Dysport®)

Botox® and Dysport® are examples of medications known as neuromodulators. When very small amounts of these medications are injected into the muscles of the face they relax wrinkles and smooth out the appearance of the skin. These neuromodulators temporarily block the signal from the nerve

that usually tells the muscle to contract. These muscle movements are the cause of wrinkling. The effects of these medications wear off after approximately 3 months. However, with regular treatments results may last longer and a smaller dose may be required.
The most common areas treated with neuromodulators are the forehead, frown lines, and crow’s feet. During your consultation with a Structure Clinic healthcare provider you can discuss any other ways that you might benefit from Botox or Dysport.

Lip Enhancement

Lip Enhancement

There are various injectable treatments that can enhance lip appearance and fullness. For a subtle enhancement Botox can be used for a "lip flip." This maintains the natural shape of the lip but relaxes the muscle that causes you to purse your lips into a thin line. It can also help reduce the appearance of those vertical lipstick lines (sometimes called smoker's lines).

Clenching /
Teeth Grinding

Clenching/Teeth Grinding

Jaw clenching and teeth grinding (bruxism) can often lead to pain and headaches. Botox® treatments can be helpful in reducing clenching and grinding. Book a complimentary assessment to find out if this treatment would be helpful for you. Often times the medication can be covered through private insurance and only an injection fee is required.

Hyperhidrosis

Hyperhidrosis (excessive sweating)

Some people experience excessive sweating which can be limiting both socially and professionally. Botox® has been well established in safely treating this condition by interrupting the signal that the nerve sends to sweat glands. Results are typically seen within 1 week and last for 6-12 months. You may be eligible for private insurance coverage for the medication.

FAQ



expand_more How many units do I need?
This is probably the most common question we encounter and is unique to everyone. The answer will depend on a few factors including: a healthcare provider’s assessment of your features, muscle strength, skin type, and most importantly - your personal goals.
That being said, here are some general estimates that may help you better understand what to expect from your treatment:
Forehead/frown lines – 25 to 50 units
Crow’s feet – 12 to 20 units

Lip flip – 4 to 8 units
expand_more How long until I see effects?
You may start to see a reduction in muscle movement and wrinkles within 3 days, but full effect is generally seen by 2 weeks after treatment.
expand_more How long will the treatment last?
Wrinkle reduction lasts approximately 3 months but may last longer with regular maintenance.
expand_more Can I go back to my activities afterwards?
Yes! You can apply makeup if you’d like go back to you day without anyone knowing about your treatment. We recommend avoiding strenuous activity for a day and avoid lying down for 4 hours after treatment. You should also avoid any hats or headbands resting on the treated area.
